Our story

First time we tried ourselves at the “Street Market”
in Ternopil. Back then all our kitchen was in a
small tent in the park. Lots of people stood in line
to try our food. It was so big that somebody had
to wait for more than half of an hour for those
noodles. Though it takes for 3 to5 minutes to
make a portion.


After a successful start, Orestes and his wife
Tatiana managed to rent a room for the new ins-
titution. Now, in the heart of the city can vary
the usual lunch Japanese cuisine.
